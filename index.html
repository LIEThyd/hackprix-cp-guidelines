Community Partner Guidelines

Welcome to the HackPrix Hackathon Community Partner Guidelines! Embark on this journey with us by following these steps to effectively promote and wholeheartedly support our exceptional Hackathon.

Code of Conduct for HackPrix Hackathon

1. Familiarize Yourself with HackPrix

Take the time to understand HackPrix's mission, goals, and any updates to the event format, schedule, themes, or challenges. Start by introducing yourself on our WhatsApp Group to connect with other community partners.

2. Promote HackPrix

Spread the word about HackPrix within your community and networks using social media, newsletters, blogs, and other communication channels to drive registrations.

4. Maintain Brand Consistency

Adhere to official logos, colors, and branding guidelines provided by HackPrix for consistent recognition and credibility. Encourage active participation by organizing pre-event meetups, webinars, or workshops.


All the HackPrix logos are here: Brand-Kit

5. Provide Support

Offer assistance and guidance to participants throughout the registration process and during the hackathon to help them make the most of their experience.

6. Uphold the Code of Conduct

Ensure all participants adhere to the HackPrix code of conduct to foster a respectful and inclusive environment. You can find the HackPrix Code of Conduct here.

7. Share Feedback

Share feedback, suggestions, or concerns with the HackPrix team for continuous improvement and future success. Celebrate achievements and successes of community members to inspire others and build community pride.

8. Stay Connected

Stay connected with the HackPrix team and community partners through the dedicated WhatsApp Group for important announcements and effective collaboration.
